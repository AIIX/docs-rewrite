<span style="color: #2c3e50;">Our formal acceptance process, undertaken before a Skill is accepted into the Marketplace. This process consists of three components:</span>

 	<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #2c3e50;"><strong>Information review</strong> – ensuring all info is accurate and understandable</span></li>
 	<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400; color: #2c3e50;"><strong>Functional review</strong> – ensuring the experience of using the Skill is intuitive</span></li>
